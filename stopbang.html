STOP BANG - Sleep Questionnaire

STOP BANG Questionnaire Instructions

Welcome to the STOP BANG Questionnaire, a tool designed to assess your risk of Obstructive Sleep Apnoea (OSA).

What You Will Need:

  • Tape Measure: You will need a tape measure to measure your neck circumference.
  • Scale and Ruler (or Height Measuring Instrument): You will either need to know your current weight and height or have the means to measure them before proceeding with the questionnaire.

How to Proceed:

  1. Read Each Question Carefully: Each question is designed to evaluate specific risk factors for Obstructive Sleep Apnoea.
  2. Provide Accurate Information: The more accurate the information, the more accurate your assessment will be.
  3. Measure Where Required: Use your tape measure, scale, and ruler for questions that require physical measurements.

Once you're ready and have all the required materials, click the "Start" button to begin the questionnaire.
